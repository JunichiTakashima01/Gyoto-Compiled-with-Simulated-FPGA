\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A spherically-\/symmetric accretion flow radially falling onto the central object. 

Density is assumed to follow a r$^\wedge$\{-\/2\} law while temperature is a power law with a specified slope.

This astrobj emits thermal synchrotron.
