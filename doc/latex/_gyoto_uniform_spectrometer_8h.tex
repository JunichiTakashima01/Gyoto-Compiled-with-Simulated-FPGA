\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Uniformly spaced spectrometers. 

Spectral channels are contiguous and uniformly spaced in either wavelength, frequency or log10 of either. \mbox{\hyperlink{class_gyoto_1_1_spectrometer_1_1_uniform}{Gyoto\+::\+Spectrometer\+::\+Uniform}} is registered four times in the factory\+: as kind=\char`\"{}wave\char`\"{}, \char`\"{}wavelog\char`\"{}, \char`\"{}freq\char`\"{} and \char`\"{}freqlog\char`\"{}.
